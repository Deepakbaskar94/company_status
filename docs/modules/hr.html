Manufacturing HR Challenges

What happens when attendance, leave, and payroll stay disconnected:

❌ Manual attendance tracking

Excel sheets and punch cards make it hard to verify actual hours worked and calculate accurate payroll.

❌ Leave approvals are slow

Multiple approval chains and no centralized tracking create delays and confusion over leave balances.

❌ Payroll calculations are error-prone

Manual component-based payroll with ESI and PF calculations without integration to actual attendance is risky.

❌ No shift or holiday visibility

Without a unified shift and holiday calendar, scheduling conflicts and incorrect overtime calculations happen.

❌ Department hierarchy is unclear

Reporting relationships, designations, and department structures aren't always consistent or documented.

❌ Role-based access is missing

Everyone sees everything, or key employee and payroll data is scattered across different people.
