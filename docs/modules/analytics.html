Analytics Challenges

What happens without real-time dashboards and KPI visibility:

❌ No executive visibility

Decision makers can't see real-time data on sales, production, or costs.

❌ KPIs are fragmented

Metrics live in spreadsheets instead of real dashboards, always out-of-date.

❌ Production pipeline stays opaque

Managers can't see which orders are on track, at risk, or overdue.

❌ Quality insights go missing

Defect trends and quality issues aren't tracked or reported at scale.

❌ Profitability is hidden

You can't see which products, customers, or regions are actually profitable.

❌ Reports take days to build

Ad hoc queries require IT support, so business asks stop coming.
