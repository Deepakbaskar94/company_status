Inventory Management Challenges

What happens when stock tracking relies on Excel and manual counts:

❌ No real-time visibility

Stock levels are outdated, making it impossible to know if materials are available for production.

❌ Batch and expiry tracking fails

Without batch management, you can't enforce FIFO (First Expire First Out) or identify expired stock in time.

❌ Stockouts stop production

Material shortages aren't detected early, causing production delays and missed delivery dates.

❌ Over-ordering wastes cash

Without MRP integration, you end up with excess stock or redundant orders that tie up working capital.

❌ Stock counts don't match

Physical inventory doesn't reconcile with system records, creating mystery shortages and write-offs.

❌ Material reservations are unclear

Multiple work orders fight over the same materials with no centralized reservation system.
