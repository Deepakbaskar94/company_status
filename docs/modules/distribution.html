Distribution Challenges

What happens without warehouse and delivery management:

❌ No warehouse visibility

Stock stored without clear locations, expiry tracking, or organization.

❌ Picking & packing errors

Manual processes lead to wrong items, quantities, or damaged goods shipping to customers.

❌ Delivery delays

No shipping coordination or tracking means customers don't know when orders arrive.

❌ Returns & disputes

Without proof of delivery, returns and customer claims go unresolved.

❌ DC costs stay hidden

Warehouse expenses aren't allocated to orders, making true delivery costs opaque.

❌ Multi-DC coordination fails

Multiple warehouses can't be managed from one system.
