Quality Management Challenges

What happens without structured quality control:

❌ Defects go undetected

Without systematic QC, quality issues surface late—after products reach customers.

❌ No inbound inspection

Incoming materials aren't inspected, so defects enter your warehouse and production.

❌ QC results aren't tracked

Pass/fail decisions are manual and don't feed back to inventory or production systems.

❌ Scrap and rework costs hide

Without QC linking to cost tracking, you can't see the true impact of quality failures.

❌ Batch status stays unclear

Materials sit in "under_qc" limbo with no clear path to availability or rejection.

❌ No root cause analysis

Quality issues repeat because there's no system to log defects or corrective actions.

Final QC
