Operations stay reactive

Teams keep switching between registers, spreadsheets, and messages. That usually means lost time, avoidable errors, and decisions made too late.

01

Manual calculations create wrong payouts, delayed salary runs, and repeated questions from workers.

Paper records go missing or are filled inconsistently, so it becomes hard to verify who worked and when.
